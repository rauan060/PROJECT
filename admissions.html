📥 Admissions at IITU

Join one of Kazakhstan’s most advanced IT universities. Whether you're applying for a Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD program — here’s what you need to know.

🧑‍🎓 Who Can Apply?

  • High school graduates (for Bachelor's)
  • University degree holders (for Master's)
  • Researchers and academics (for PhD)

📄 Required Documents

  • Completed online application form
  • Passport or national ID
  • Educational certificates and transcripts
  • English language proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL – if available)
  • Motivation letter (for Master’s and PhD)

🌐 How to Apply

  1. Visit the official website: iitu.edu.kz
  2. Choose your desired program
  3. Fill out the application form online
  4. Upload all required documents
  5. Wait for confirmation from the admissions office

📆 Important Dates

  • Application period: June – August
  • Entrance exams: July
  • Semester starts: September

💰 Tuition & Scholarships

IITU offers competitive tuition fees and various scholarship opportunities based on merit and financial need.
